1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

  1. During the Term of this Agreement, the Company shall collaborate with the Partner for assistance in recruitment and selection of qualified candidates for healthcare sector, as per the requirements outlined by the Company's clients from time to time ("Services").
  2. The Partner shall, based on vacancies of the Company from time to time, conduct candidate searches and screening processes to identify potential candidates that match the Company's job descriptions and specifications through a combination of external advertising, in house search of its data repository and through other sourcing channels. Upon identifying suitable candidates, the Partner shall conduct preliminary screening, assessment and provide the Company with candidate profiles that are short-listed and include relevant qualifications, experience, and other necessary information.
  3. The Company shall inform the partner about the duplicate profiles from the submissions by checking in the application database available with the company from last one year period. All the new profiles are to be shared with its clients and based on the communication received from its clients, share the details of the shortlisted candidates. On receiving the shortlisted candidate profiles from the Company, the Partner in collaboration with the Company shall organize, coordinate physical interviews where the candidates are required in consultation with the Company and/or the Company's clients or arrange for online interviews in order to select the suitable candidate. It is hereby agreed that all costs related to the onsite interview process (Physical Interviews), including venue booking, food, and conveyance will be discussed on case to case basis.
  4. Upon selection of a suitable candidate, the offers shall be extended by the HR or hiring manager of the Company's client to the Company and the Company shall provide the same to the Partner for notifying all selected candidates with a letter of appointment provided by the Company's client. The Partner shall follow-up with the selected candidate and keep him/her on the loop with respect to the developments, post offer acceptance till on-boarding. Notwithstanding the foregoing, it is hereby clarified that neither the Partner nor the Company shall be responsible for complying with the applicable labour laws in relation to the selected candidate. All such labour law compliances shall be taken care by the Company's clients.
  5. The Partner hereby agrees and acknowledges that in the event a selected candidate leaves the employment during the probation period and the Company's client provides sufficient evidence regarding the termination of the employment of a candidate, no Service Fee shall be payable to the Partner in relation to such candidate. Additionally, if the Partner receives the Service Fee directly from the selected candidate, they are required to remit 50% of the Service Fee to the Company in the event of such termination of the employment of the selected candidate.

2. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

2.1. Roles and responsibilities of the Company:

The roles and responsibilities of the Company shall include the following:

  1. Provide a clear and detailed description of the job, including essential qualifications, experience and responsibilities along with details of compensation to offer, to enable the Partner to perform the search effectively;
  2. Exercising abundant care and caution in handling the bio-data and information provided by the Partner such that no unauthorized use or misuse is made of the same;
  3. Timely communicate all the decisions and feedback as given by its clients to the Company;
  4. On selection, Company shall communicate a complete break-up of the salary offered to the selected candidates as informed by its clients to enable the Partner to raise an invoice in a case where the client pays the recruitment fee.
  5. The Company shall provide the appointment letter, visa document or any such necessary documents in respect of all selected candidates to the Partner as provided by its clients.

2.2. Roles and responsibilities of the Partner:

The roles and responsibilities of the Partner shall include the following:

  1. Conduct thorough searches to identify potential candidates who meet the client's job descriptions and qualifications.
  2. Give a proper briefing to the candidates regarding the job role, job requirements, responsibilities, proposed pay and all other clarifications as deemed necessary;
  3. Conduct a background verification on the candidates before referring to the Company and make sure all the information is true;
  4. Provide the candidate's resume, work profile and all other information and documents as required by the Company; and
  5. Arrange and coordinate all meetings, interviews and/or discussions as may be required by the Company.
  6. To keep the candidate warm and not drop from the process.
  7. Ensure that the candidate satisfies all requirements as required under the applicable local laws from where the candidates are being hired and is eligible under the law to be appointed as an employee of the Company's client as communicated from time to time.
  8. Collect Service Fee directly from the selected candidate or any other payment only with the prior written acceptance from the Company and/or the Company's client.
